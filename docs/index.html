Webpage Showcase

Stop wrestling with your mouse to get the perfect scroll speed. Turn your browser into a professional camera dolly for polished product demo videos.

Webpage Showcase is a Chrome extension that automates cinematic vertical scrolling on any webpage — so you can screen-record landing pages, docs, and marketing sites with consistent, tunable timing every time.

It is not a screen recorder. Use it with OBS, Loom, QuickTime, or any recorder you already love.
