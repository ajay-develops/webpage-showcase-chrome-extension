Stop wrestling with your mouse to get the perfect scroll speed.

Webpage Showcase turns your browser into a professional camera dolly, giving you cinematic, repeatable, and tunable scrolls every single time — so your landing page demos look polished on the first take.

★ WHAT IT DOES

Webpage Showcase automates vertical scrolling on any webpage in a smooth, predictable sequence:

• Fast-scroll to each section
• Brief pause at the section start
• Slow, cinematic scroll through the entire section
• Optional pause at the section end
• Jump to the next section — skipping awkward gaps

Press Esc anytime to stop. A small on-page overlay shows tour progress without getting in your way.

★ NOT A SCREEN RECORDER

Webpage Showcase does not record video or audio. It only controls scroll timing on the page. Use it alongside OBS, Loom, QuickTime, Screen Studio, or any screen recorder you already use.

★ WHO IT'S FOR

• Founders recording product walkthroughs
• Marketers creating landing page promo videos
• Developers demoing docs or marketing sites
• Anyone tired of inconsistent manual scrolling

★ KEY FEATURES

AUTO-DETECT SECTIONS
Open a site for the first time and Webpage Showcase finds logical sections automatically — hero, features, footer, and more.

PER-WEBSITE CONFIGURATION
Settings are saved per hostname. Tune once for stripe.com, once for your own site — configs persist across sessions.

FULL CONTROL IN OPTIONS
Reorder sections, rename labels, edit CSS selectors, and fine-tune timing:
• Header offset for sticky navbars
• Fast vs slow scroll speeds
• Pause durations (global and per-section)
• Countdown before the tour starts

QUICK SETTINGS IN THE POPUP
Adjust header offset, scroll speed, and countdown without leaving the page.

EXPORT & IMPORT
Share JSON configs between machines or teammates.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
All data stays in local browser storage. No analytics, no telemetry, no cloud sync, no external servers.

★ HOW TO USE

1. Open any multi-section webpage
2. Click the Webpage Showcase icon
3. Click Start Showcase (optional countdown gives you time to hit Record)
4. Record with your favorite screen recorder
5. Press Esc to stop early if needed

Optional: open Configure Sections to perfect the tour before your final take.

★ RECOMMENDED RECORDING SETUP

• Viewport: 1440×900 or 1280×800
• Start recording after clicking Start Showcase
• Hide cookie banners on sites you control with data-showcase-hide
